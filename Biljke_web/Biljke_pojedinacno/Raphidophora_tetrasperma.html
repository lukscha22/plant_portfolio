Raphidophora tetrasperma

Named for their resemblance to the magnificent Monstera deliciosa, the mini monstera (Rhaphidophora tetrasperma) is actually not a monstera but a part of an independent genus, Rhaphidophora. This genus, which contains approximately 100 different species originates from tropical regions in Africa and Asia and is a part of the Araceae family, making Rhaphidophora plants distantly related to those in the Monstera genus.
